The Process

  1. **Initial Inquiry:** Fill out the contact form below with your request. Please include your reference photo and desired size.
  2. **Quote & Agreement:** I will review your request and send a detailed quote and timeline. A 50% deposit is required to begin the work.
  3. **Sketching & Approval:** I'll begin the sketch and send you progress photos for your feedback and approval before finalization.
  4. **Completion & Delivery:** Once complete, I'll send a final photo. After the remaining payment, the artwork will be carefully packaged and shipped to you.

About the Artist

Hello! I am a self-taught artist with a deep passion for capturing the human spirit through pencil and charcoal. For me, a portrait isn't just a drawing; it's a window into a person's life, a reflection of their emotions and personality. I believe every face tells a unique story, and my mission is to tell that story with sensitivity and detail. I look forward to bringing your memories and loved ones to life on paper.
